SPECIAL FEATURES

What Makes Us Special

Welcome to our exceptional school! Here are the special features that set us apart

Academic Excellence

Our school is renowned for its commitment to academic excellence. With a rigorous curriculum, experienced faculty, and a supportive learning environment, we strive to nurture the intellectual growth and academic success of every student.

  • Experienced and qualified faculty
  • Comprehensive curriculum
  • Regular assessments and feedback
  • Individual attention to students

Sports Facilities

We believe in physical fitness and sports excellence. Our school provides excellent sports facilities including cricket, football, basketball, volleyball, and athletics.

  • Multi-purpose sports ground
  • Indoor games facilities
  • Professional sports coaches
  • Regular sports competitions

Martial Arts

Martial arts training develops self-discipline, confidence, and self-defense skills, helping build character and physical strength.

  • Trained martial arts instructors
  • Focus on discipline and respect
  • Self-defense techniques
  • Character building through practice

Extra-curricular Activities

Wide range of activities including music, dance, art, drama, and debate for overall personality development.

  • Music and dance classes
  • Art and craft workshops
  • Drama and theater
  • Debate and public speaking

Air-Conditioned Classrooms

All classrooms are air-conditioned for comfortable learning environment, ensuring better concentration and productivity.

  • Comfortable temperature control
  • Better concentration environment
  • Enhanced learning experience
  • All-season comfort

Smart Classrooms with Digital Panels

Equipped with digital panels and interactive tools for engaging and effective education through technology.

  • Interactive digital boards
  • Audio-visual learning aids
  • Digital learning resources
  • Technology-enabled teaching

Hostel Facilities

Safe and comfortable hostel with supervision, nutritious food, and homely environment for outstation students.

  • Separate hostels for boys and girls
  • 24/7 security and supervision
  • Nutritious and hygienic food
  • Study rooms and recreation areas

A Message of Excellence

Principal

St. Johns Public School

"

St. Johns public school is a convent that has been established to provide the children ample opportunities to grow and progress in a modern and scientific way.

The Institution is equipped with ultra modern teaching aids and facilities. We at St. John's Public school strive to nurture the young mind in a way that enables them to achieve excellence not only in academic pursuit but also in every aspect of life by keeping close contact with parents.

We believe not only in the ability of Spoken English but also in overall development of personality of the child and lay emphasis on character-building, discipline, team work-spirit and moral values.

I hope our sincere, honest and dedicated efforts will help this institution grow in totality to remarkable heights.

St. Johns Public School
